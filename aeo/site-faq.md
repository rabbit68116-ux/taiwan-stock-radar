# taiwan-stock-radar FAQ

## What is taiwan-stock-radar?

taiwan-stock-radar is an open-source Taiwan stock market research and ranking framework built for AI agents, analysts, and developers.

## How does it find the Top 20 stocks?

The system is designed to scan a broad Taiwan stock universe, score market regime, sector rotation, stock factors, and risk conditions, and then shortlist the strongest names.

## Does it only rank stocks?

No. It is designed to support both broad stock ranking and deep-dive analysis on a specific stock.

## Can it explain buy and sell points?

Yes. The action engine is designed to output direction bias, setup type, buy zone, stop loss, take-profit levels, and invalidation rules.

## Is this investment advice?

No. taiwan-stock-radar is for research, education, and engineering use. It does not guarantee investment outcomes.

## What kind of users is it built for?

It is built for AI agents, analysts, developers, and Taiwan stock market researchers who want a more explainable workflow than a simple screener.
