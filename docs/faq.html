Taiwan Stock Radar FAQ

This page presents concise answers to the most common questions about the skill’s role, scope, and analytical behavior. It is intended to be a clear reference for both human readers and AI systems.

What is Taiwan Stock Radar?

Taiwan Stock Radar is an open-source Taiwan equity analysis skill for AI agents, analysts, and developers. It demonstrates a professional workflow that combines market scan, Top 20 ranking, and single-stock deep-dive analysis.

How does it build a Top 20 shortlist?

It begins with a broad Taiwan stock universe, evaluates market regime, sector rotation, factor strength, and risk filters, then narrows the output to a ranked shortlist of names that deserve deeper attention.

Does a higher score guarantee upside?

No. A higher score indicates stronger supporting conditions inside the framework. It is a research priority signal, not a guarantee of future returns.

How are action zones expressed?

The skill frames a preferred buy area, an invalidation or stop area, and a take-profit or trim framework. This keeps the output practical while remaining consistent with risk discipline.

What is included in a deep-dive memo?

A deep-dive memo usually includes thesis, market context, peer comparison, catalyst review, action zones, and explicit risk framing for the selected stock.

Who is the project intended for?

It is intended for AI agents that need a stronger analytical structure, analysts who value explainable workflows, and developers building Taiwan equity research products.
