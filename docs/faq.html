關於 Taiwan Stock Radar 的常見問題 Common questions about Taiwan Stock Radar

這一頁整理 Taiwan Stock Radar `v1.4` 最常被問到的核心問題，說明這個單股指標研究 skill 怎麼運作、會產出什麼，以及它的邊界在哪裡。

This page answers the most common questions about Taiwan Stock Radar `v1.4`, including how the single-stock indicator workflow operates, what it returns, and where its limits are.

什麼是 Taiwan Stock Radar？ What is Taiwan Stock Radar?

它是一個專門用來研究單一台股的 AI skill。它不只給一句看多或看空，而是模擬一場正式研究會議，輸出帶有訊號引擎、風格權重與策略模組的決策文件。

It is an AI skill built for one Taiwan stock at a time. Instead of a loose bullish or bearish comment, it produces a reviewable decision packet with signal engines, style weights, and strategy modules.

為什麼要用多位 specialist agent？ Why use multiple specialist agents?

因為一種分析語氣很容易忽略其他風險。把趨勢、動能、量價、籌碼、基本面、事件、風控、策略選擇與驗證拆開後，結論會更平衡，也更像專業投研團隊。

Because one analytical voice can hide important contradictions. Separating trend, momentum, price-volume, flow, fundamentals, events, risk, strategy selection, and validation creates a more balanced result.

主持 agent 扮演什麼角色？ What does the chair agent do?

主持 agent 不是另一個分析師，而是會議主持人。它負責整理共識、標出分歧、做權重整合，最後形成正式結論。

The chair agent is not just another analyst. It moderates the meeting, surfaces disagreement, applies weighting, and writes the final verdict.

買賣點會怎麼表達？ How are buy and sell points expressed?

輸出不只是一句「可以買」，而是會定義 signal-engine summary、style profile、策略模組、建議買入區、替代進場條件、停損、停利階梯與失效條件，把判斷連回可執行的交易紀律。

The output defines the signal-engine summary, style profile, strategy module, preferred buy zone, alternate entry triggers, stop-loss, take-profit ladder, and invalidation so the thesis stays connected to execution discipline.

v1.4 多了什麼？ What is new in v1.4?

`v1.4` 新增了六層訊號引擎、交易風格權重、最小可用欄位集，以及更完整的台股指標白皮書。

`v1.4` adds six signal engines, trading-style weights, a minimum viable field set, and a fuller Taiwan-equity indicator whitepaper.

如果 agents 彼此意見不同怎麼辦？ What happens when agents disagree?

分歧會被保留下來，而不是被一句結論硬蓋掉。這也是為什麼最終文件會同時呈現共識與 dissent memo。

Disagreement is preserved rather than erased. That is why the final packet shows both consensus and dissent notes.

這個專案適合誰？ Who is this project for?

它適合想建立更嚴謹台股研判流程的 AI agent、需要可解釋輸出的分析者，以及要把投研方法產品化的開發者。

It is designed for AI agents that need a stronger Taiwan-stock workflow, analysts who value explainable reasoning, and builders productizing research methods.
