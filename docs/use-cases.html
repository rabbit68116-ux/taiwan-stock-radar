Taiwan Stock Radar 在實務上怎麼使用 How Taiwan Stock Radar is applied in practice

Taiwan Stock Radar 不只服務一種場景。相同的單股研究框架，可以依需求切換成快速研判、正式委員會備忘錄、進場前審查與事後檢討。

Taiwan Stock Radar is not tied to one scenario. The same single-stock research framework can be adapted for quick judgment, full committee memos, pre-trade review, and post-call evaluation.

單股快速研判 Single-stock judgment

當使用者只想知道某檔股票目前偏多還是偏空、重要風險是什麼、可否規劃進場時，系統可以在保留紀律的前提下給出精簡版決策。

When a user needs a fast answer on one ticker, the council can return a compact but disciplined verdict with directional bias, key evidence, and actionable zones.

深度委員會備忘錄 Deep-dive committee memo

當需要更高品質的研究輸出時，系統會擴展成完整研究備忘錄，包含各專家簡報、分歧整理、情境樹與更詳細的操作計畫。

When a higher-quality research note is needed, the skill expands into a full memo with specialist briefs, disagreement review, scenario framing, and a detailed action plan.

進場前審查 Pre-trade review

在真正進場前，委員會可以檢查技術結構、籌碼、事件時點與風險是否同時支持這個個案，而不是只靠單一理由下判斷。

Before entry, the council tests whether structure, flow, event timing, and risk all support the setup strongly enough to justify action.

事後檢討 Post-call review

當原先 thesis 已經發展一段時間後，系統可以回頭比對初始邏輯與後續市場行為，找出哪些專家訊號最有價值。

After the original thesis has played out, the skill can compare the initial logic with later market behavior and identify which specialist signals mattered most.

免責聲明： 使用情境展示的是研究流程如何被應用，不代表任何情境下都應直接採取實際交易行動。Disclaimer: These use cases illustrate how the research workflow can be applied. They should not be read as a direct instruction to place live trades.
