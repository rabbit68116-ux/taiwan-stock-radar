Taiwan Stock Radar 如何執行單股研究會議 How Taiwan Stock Radar runs a single-stock research council

這一頁說明 Taiwan Stock Radar `v1.2` 的核心分析序列。整個流程的目標不是產生更華麗的觀點，而是讓單股判斷有更嚴謹的拆解、辯論與整合過程。

This page outlines the analytical sequence behind Taiwan Stock Radar `v1.2`. The goal is not louder certainty, but a more rigorous way to decompose, challenge, and synthesize single-stock judgment.

1. 個案設定 1. Case setup

流程從定義一檔股票、一個分析日期、一個研究週期與一個明確問題開始。這能避免討論失焦，也避免 agent 用模糊問題產生模糊答案。

The process starts by defining one stock, one analysis date, one time horizon, and one explicit research question. That keeps the meeting focused and prevents vague answers.

2. 台股脈絡校準 2. Taiwan market context

在下方向判斷之前，系統會先看市場 regime、所屬族群、同業相對位置與事件時點。單一股票不能脫離它所在的市場背景來理解。

Before any directional call is made, the system reviews market regime, sector position, peer context, and event timing. A stock should not be judged in isolation.

3. Specialist 獨立簡報 3. Independent specialist briefs

技術面、籌碼面、基本面、催化因子與風控角色會先各自形成初步看法。這能避免第一個強勢敘事過早主導整場會議。

Technical, chip-flow, fundamental, catalyst, and risk specialists each produce an initial view independently so that the first strong narrative does not dominate the whole meeting.

4. 交叉質詢 4. Cross-examination

主持 agent 會比對各角色的觀點，找出主要衝突與不確定性，讓問題被攤開，而不是讓矛盾在最後摘要中消失。

The chair agent compares specialist views, highlights major conflicts, and surfaces the highest-priority uncertainty instead of letting contradictions disappear in the final summary.

5. 權重整合 5. Weighted synthesis

最終結論不是投票平均，而是依專業角色與指標權重做綜合判斷。技術面很強時可以提高信心，但事件風險或風控反對仍應下修結論強度。

The final verdict is not a simple vote. It is built through weighted judgment, where strong technicals may raise conviction while catalyst risk or risk objections can still reduce confidence.

6. 決策文件輸出 6. Decision packet

最後輸出會包含 thesis、base/bull/bear 三情境、建議買入區、進場條件、停損、停利階梯、失效條件，以及保留下來的 dissent memo。

The final packet includes the thesis, base/bull/bear scenarios, preferred buy zone, entry triggers, stop-loss, take-profit ladder, invalidation, and preserved dissent notes.
