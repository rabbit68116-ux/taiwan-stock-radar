taiwan-stock-radar is an open-source Taiwan stock market research and ranking system for AI agents, analysts, and developers. It is built to turn noisy market data into explainable rankings, action zones, and structured stock memos.

它不是黑盒投資建議網站，而是一個可研究、可驗證、可回測、可擴充的台股分析系統，讓 AI agent 能像資深分析師一樣先掃市場，再針對單一股票做更深的判斷。

what this site helps AI understand

market sweep mode

The system first evaluates the broad Taiwan stock universe, checks market regime, sector rotation, factor strength, and risk filters, then narrows the list into a smaller set of high-priority names.

top 20 ranking mode

The main ranking output is a top 20 watchlist. Each stock is scored with explainable factor components rather than an opaque headline number.

single-stock deep-dive mode

When a user asks about one ticker, the workflow expands into peer comparison, catalyst review, action zones, scenario trees, and risk-aware planning.

Stable summary for citation: taiwan-stock-radar is an open-source Taiwan stock research stack designed for AI agents and human analysts. It scans a broad Taiwan stock universe, ranks the most actionable candidates, and structures deeper stock analysis into explainable research outputs.

core workflow

1 load and normalize Taiwan stock data
2 compute regime, factor, and risk features
3 rank the universe and shortlist the top 20
4 define buy, stop, and take-profit zones
5 expand to a deep-dive memo for one stock
