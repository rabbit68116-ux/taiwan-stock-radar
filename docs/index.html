台股單股指標研究技能Single-stock indicator committee skill for Taiwan equities

台灣股票雷達：讓八位專家級 AI 分析師對同一檔股票完成正式研討、訊號評估與風格化決策 Taiwan Stock Radar turns one stock into a formal research committee with eight specialist AI analysts.

Taiwan Stock Radar 以單一台股為核心，不做空泛摘要，而是讓趨勢、動能、量價、籌碼、基本面、事件、策略與驗證角色先各自發言，再由主持 agent 整合出正式結論。

Taiwan Stock Radar is built around one Taiwan stock at a time. Instead of a shallow summary, it lets trend, momentum, price-volume, chip-flow, fundamental, event, strategy, and validation roles speak independently before a chair agent synthesizes the final verdict.

最終輸出是一份可執行的單股決策文件，包含訊號引擎摘要、風格權重、策略模組、趨勢預測、買入區、停損區、停利區、失效條件，以及會議中保留下來的反方意見。

The final output is an execution-aware decision packet with signal-engine summaries, style weights, strategy modules, trend scenarios, buy zones, stop-loss, targets, invalidation rules, and preserved dissent.

專案定位 Project Statement

Taiwan Stock Radar 展示的是一種更成熟的股市 AI skill 工作方式。`v1.4` 把單股判斷先收斂成台股訊號引擎，再用風格權重與策略模組整合成可執行結論。

Taiwan Stock Radar demonstrates a more mature stock-AI workflow. In `v1.4`, it turns single-stock judgment into a Taiwan-market signal stack first, then applies style weights and strategy modules before producing an actionable conclusion.

不是雜訊 watchlist，而是一檔股票 One stock, not a noisy watchlist

`v1.4` 明確聚焦於一檔 TWSE 或 TPEX 股票，把單股判斷當成主體，並要求先看訊號堆疊，再談策略與執行。

`v1.4` is explicitly centered on one TWSE or TPEX stock at a time, treating single-stock judgment as the main event and requiring a signal-stack review before strategy and execution language.

多位 specialist AI analyst Multiple specialist AI analysts

趨勢、動能、量價、籌碼、基本面、事件、策略與驗證先被分開，再交由主持 agent 整合成最終結論。

Trend, momentum, price-volume, chip-flow, fundamental, event, strategy, and validation perspectives are separated first, then synthesized by a chair agent into the final verdict.

保留分歧，而不是掩蓋矛盾 Visible disagreement

混合訊號應該降低信心，而不是被強行包成一段很順的敘事。這也是為什麼 final packet 會保留 dissent memo。

Mixed evidence should lower confidence instead of being flattened into a smooth narrative. That is why the final packet preserves dissent.

輸出以決策為中心 Decision-oriented output

結果不只是一句方向判斷，而是訊號引擎、風格權重、策略模組、情境分支、買入區、停損、停利階梯與 thesis-break 條件。

The result is more than a directional opinion. It includes signal engines, style weights, strategy modules, scenarios, buy zones, stop-loss logic, take-profit ladders, and thesis-break conditions.

v1.4 指標與風格版本 v1.4 Indicator and Style Layer

這一版的核心不是增加更多形容詞，而是讓每個單股結論都多出「訊號是否健康」與「風格是否匹配」兩道門。

The point of this release is not more adjectives. It is to add two extra gates to every single-stock call: signal health and style fit.

六層訊號引擎 Six signal engines

系統先把單股拆成趨勢、動能、量價、籌碼、基本面與事件六層，再決定這個 setup 是否值得往下做。

The system first breaks a stock into six layers: trend, momentum, price-volume, chip flow, fundamentals, and events, then decides whether the setup deserves further action.

風格權重切換 Style-weight switching

同一檔股票在短線、波段與中長線下不應用相同標準。`v1.4` 會依風格切換權重與優先欄位。

The same stock should not be judged by the same lens across short-term, swing, and position trading. `v1.4` switches both weights and field priority by style.

最小可用欄位集 Minimum viable field set

如果資料有限，系統至少能用 `20MA`、`60MA`、量能、`MACD`、`RSI`、外資、投信與月營收 `YoY` 做出穩定初判。

If data is limited, the system can still form a stable first read with `20MA`, `60MA`, volume, `MACD`, `RSI`, foreign flow, trust flow, and monthly revenue `YoY`.

台股實戰語言 Taiwan-practical language

像「`60MA` 之上找買點」、「回檔量縮」、「投信連買」、「事件窗口降權」這些，現在都被正式寫進 skill。

Practical Taiwan-market rules such as “prefer entries above `60MA`,” “pullback with contraction,” “trust-buy streaks,” and “event-window downgrades” are now formalized in the skill.

Specialist Agent Council Specialist Agent Council

系統使用帶權重的研究委員會，讓任何一種分析風格都不會單獨控制最終結論，並把策略選擇與驗證獨立成可見角色。

The system uses a weighted research committee so that no single analytical style controls the final conclusion, while strategy selection and validation remain visible roles.

Agent 角色 Agent 主要責任 Primary responsibility 權重 Weight
Chief Strategist 主持會議、裁決衝突並撰寫最終綜合結論。Leads the meeting, resolves conflicts, and writes the final synthesis. 0.18
Technical Strategist 評估多週期趨勢、支撐壓力與量價結構。Judges multi-timeframe structure, support, resistance, and volume confirmation. 0.16
Chip Flow Analyst 觀察外資、投信、融資與籌碼分配風險。Evaluates foreign flow, trust flow, financing pressure, and distribution risk. 0.14
Fundamental Analyst 檢視月營收、獲利品質、產品週期與商業敘事。Tests revenue quality, earnings support, product cycle, and business narrative. 0.14
Catalyst Analyst 追蹤法說、營收公告、政策與產業事件時點。Tracks earnings, revenue-release windows, policy timing, and industry events. 0.10
Risk Manager 從流動性、波動與失效紀律挑戰 thesis。Challenges the thesis through liquidity, volatility, and invalidation discipline. 0.11

代表性輸出 Representative Output

一個成熟的股市 skill 應該交付的不是一句方向判斷，而是能被檢討、被質疑、也能被執行的研究成果。

A serious stock skill should deliver more than a directional opinion. It should produce work that can be reviewed, challenged, and acted on with discipline.

研究文件 Research packet

  • 股票背景：市場、族群、同業位置與目前 regime。Stock context: market, sector, peer map, and current regime.
  • Specialist notes：技術、籌碼、基本面、催化與風險分開陳述。Specialist notes: separate technical, chip-flow, fundamental, catalyst, and risk briefs.
  • Strategy engine：primary / secondary strategy 與避免採用的策略。Strategy engine: primary and secondary strategy plus the strategy to avoid.
  • Validation panel：績效、尾部風險與穩健度檢查。Validation panel: performance, tail-risk, and robustness review.
  • Scenario tree：base、bull、bear 與觸發條件。Scenario tree: base case, bull case, and bear case with trigger conditions.
  • Consensus map：哪些地方有共識，哪些仍有爭議。Consensus map: what the council agrees on and what remains contested.

操作計畫 Action plan

  • 建議買入區：何處風險報酬比開始變得合理。Preferred buy zone: where risk-reward becomes acceptable.
  • 進場觸發：積極進場與保守確認的差異。Entry triggers: aggressive entry versus conservative confirmation.
  • 風險控制：停損、失效條件與事件風險註記。Risk control: stop-loss, invalidation, and event-risk caveats.
  • 出場框架：TP1、TP2 與分批調節邏輯。Exit framework: TP1, TP2, and trim logic tied to scenario quality.
